Welcome to Cleanliness Forum

Cleanliness Forum is maintained and developed by Government of India. This provides citizens of India a feature, to make the India clean and beautiful. This program is an extension of Sawachh Bharat Abhiyan.

Cleanliness forum

A small step from the end of every Indian citizen can be a big step for fulfilling the purpose of the ‘Clean India’ campaign. We should teach the purpose and importance of the cleanliness in our daily lives to our kids. Cleanliness Forum provides online image based complaint portal to keep our surroundings neat and clean. This helps you in making your surrounding clean simply by taking an image of the dirty area or unhygienic place and uploading it on the forum to get it clean. This also helps in checking the status of complaint of the unhygienic area, getting an update on the complaint.
